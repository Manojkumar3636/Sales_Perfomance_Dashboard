                                              Sales Performance Dashboard

Overview :
This interactive dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance,
enabling users to track key metrics, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

Features :

Sales Representative:
            Filter sales data by individual representatives to evaluate performance.
Product: 
            Analyze sales by product category to identify top-selling items.
Total Amount:    
            Track total sales revenue over time.
Sales Trend:
            Visualize sales trends over time to identify patterns and seasonality.
Location: 
            Examine sales performance by geographic location to identify regional strengths and weaknesses.
